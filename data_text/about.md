This application visualizes geochemical data from more than 2,000 seawater samples collected around Japan (Kodama et al., 2024). Users can explore parameters such as salinity, hydrogen and oxygen isotopes, time period, and location, and easily download high-quality figures. Interactive 3D/4D visualization is also available.

As of 2025, the database has grown to include nearly 50,000 seawater isotope records, integrating global datasets such as the NASA GISS database and the CoralHydro2k seawater isotope database (Atwood et al., 2026).

The platform enables consistent and quantitative comparison of seawater isotope data across regions and time periods using a unified analytical framework.

The Japan-region dataset consists of samples analyzed under consistent conditions, ensuring high comparability across locations and time periods. Additional datasets from the Northwest Pacific are being continuously integrated.

All figures can be freely downloaded and used. Please cite the original data sources, as listed in `Data Sources` and `Selected Data`, as well as this application: [https://envgeo.h.kyoto-u.ac.jp/sw_jpn/](https://envgeo.h.kyoto-u.ac.jp/sw_jpn/) by T. Ishimura. (Mar. 2026)
